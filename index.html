Frequently Asked Questions

Is your data secure and private?

Yes. All call recordings and transcripts are encrypted. Your data is never shared with third parties or used for training. You can request deletion at any time.

Can I keep my existing phone number?

Yes. You can port your existing number to us, or we'll set up a new one in your area code. Number porting takes 2-5 business days. We handle the paperwork.

What happens if the AI can't answer a question?

You provide the script and FAQs. If something falls outside that scope, the AI takes a message and you follow up. No call is ever dropped without a recap sent to you and the caller.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. No contracts, no setup fees, no cancellation fees. Cancel from your dashboard and you keep service until the end of your billing period.

How does this compare to hiring a receptionist?

A full-time receptionist costs $3,000-$4,000/month and works 9-5 with breaks and vacation. Our AI works 24/7/365, never calls in sick, sends instant SMS and email recaps, and costs as little as $37/month. For the price of one month of a human receptionist, you get a full year of AI coverage.

What if I run out of minutes mid-month?

You can add a Bonus Minute Pack at any time — 100 min for $15 or 200 min for $25. That's as low as $0.13/min, far cheaper than standard overage.
